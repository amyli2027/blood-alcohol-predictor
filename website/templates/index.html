What Different BAC Levels Mean:

  • 0.02 - 0.03: Relaxed, slightly warm feeling
  • 0.04 - 0.06: Tipsy, lowered inhibitions, slight impairment
  • 0.07 - 0.09: Legally impaired (driving illegal), noticeable slurring and imbalance
  • 0.10 - 0.12: Drunk, loss of judgment and motor skills
  • 0.13 - 0.20: Very drunk, emotional swings, possible vomiting
  • 0.21 - 0.29: Blackout zone, severe impairment
  • 0.30+: Potential unconsciousness, medical emergency
